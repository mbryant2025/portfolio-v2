Overview

PyEquations is a Python package for solving a bunch of equations with a bunch of unknowns.

PyEquations maximally solves with the given equations, functions, and variables. You can simply dump all your equations and the known values; PyEquations will figure out the rest (including units)!

More

In addition to these basic features, there are many more advanced capabilities and checks performed by PyEquations. This includes recursive branching, solution validation and automatic branch deletion, and multi-level inheritance.
