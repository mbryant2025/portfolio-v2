Overview

Made for HackDuke2023, PatientWatch is a patient monitoring system. It is a modular system that is currently fitted for tracking patient temperature, room humidity, and patient movement.

The system works over the internet, and is designed to be used in a hospital setting. During the Hackathon, we streamed data from accross campus and we won first place.

Video

Details

Web hosting is made possible through a Flask server. The software was made for a Raspberry Pi, but can also be run on a PC. For the case of a PC, an Arduino is used to collect data from sensors and send it to the PC, with use of the PySerial library. A webcam is attached to the computer where a simple OpenCV algorithm is used to detect movement.

Role

This project was completed by a group of three students for HackDuke2023 and I was responsible for the software of the project.
